Virtual Terminal Integration

The CSI Virtual Terminal (VT) Solution simplifies the task of processing customer credit cards by reducing the amount of steps required to take a payment. The CSI VT solution allows the POS tax clerk to process a payment via a CSI QuickPay button embedded within the ACT Web or Thomson Reuters interface. The transaction is then completed without exiting the program.
