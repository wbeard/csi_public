Upfront and Recurring Costs

$0… Period!

CSI makes essentially 100% of our revenue from transaction fees. Initial installation and ongoing general maintenance will be performed without any costs to the client.

While this arrangement is financially beneficial to the client, it also ensures that the client can trust that CSI will always be highly motivated to ensure that the client’s payment processing system is always operating in an optimal manner.

If the client isn’t able to process transactions in a convenient and correct manner, CSI doesn’t make money… Period!

Transaction Fee Options

CSI supports ‘Absorbed’ fee models wherein the fee is absorbed by the client, as well as, ‘Convenience’ fee models wherein the fee is passed on to the paying customer.

Fees may be defined in the form of percentage-rate fees that vary with the amount of the transaction or as fixed-rate fees that remain constant regardless of the transaction amount.

CSI will work with the client to define the model that is most cost effective to their organization, as well as, to the customers they serve.
