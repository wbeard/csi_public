CSI : The Exceptional Solution

The CSI Solution provides our clients with a myriad of tools to manage their payment activity.

Accessed by the client via a dedicated Management Console account, clients may execute searches, process credits and voids, manage users, and view over 70 various real-time updated reports each focusing on topics, such as, transaction detail, settlement and/or remittance activity, and exception occurrence.

All reports are able to be organized by payment method, payment type, collection mode, and organization unit providing for one of the most comprehensive payment activity reporting modules in the industry.

Integration Methods

The CSI Fully Hosted Solution with real-time integration capabilities for validating incoming customers, as well as, for updating customer specific CIS systems.

The CSI Virtual Terminal Solution that enables our clients to validate and manage their clients upon entry into a client hosted store-front while ultimately ‘posting’ the customer into a CSI hosted Virtual Terminal Solution for payment information collection and processing.

For more information concerning the CSI Virtual Terminal Solution, click here.

The CSI eCollections Portal Solution that enables a client hosted end-to-end store-front to execute processing requests to CSI while maintaining ownership of the overall customer session.
